Register Your Child

PEM welcomes children with any disability — pan-disability, all abilities welcome — under the age of 18. There's no cost to join, no long-term commitment, and most sessions need no booking at all: just turn up and play.

  • ✔Completely free, always — paid for by charitable donations
  • ✔Siblings and parents welcome on the field to support their child
  • ✔DBS-checked, FA-qualified volunteer coaches
  • ✔Robust safeguarding policies to keep every child safe
Register Your Child (Free)

How registration works

  1. 1Email us at contact@pemdcc.co.uk with your child's name, age, and a little about their needs.
  2. 2We'll add you to our private Facebook group and WhatsApp community for session updates.
  3. 3Check the Diary of Events and just turn up to your first free session!

Volunteer roles include:

  • ⚽ Sports Coach & Helper (we support your FA certificates)
  • 🤝 Volunteer Helper (setup, games, events, fundraising)
  • ☕ Hospitality & refreshments
  • 🛡️ Safeguarding & welfare support
  • 🎓 Youth work experience placements

No commitment required — come and see if it's for you, no strings attached.

About Us

Run by veterans. Powered by volunteers. Built for every child.

How it all started

Founded by two Royal Navy veterans, PEM Disability Children's Club was born from a simple but powerful idea: service doesn't end when the uniform comes off. After dedicating over 40 years of service between them, our founders Lee and Luke sought a new mission — one rooted in compassion, community, and opportunity.

What began as a grassroots effort has grown into a thriving volunteer-led sports club designed specifically for children with disabilities. We believe every child, regardless of physical or cognitive ability, deserves the chance to play, grow, and thrive through sport. Our club offers a safe, inclusive, and joyful environment where children can build confidence, make friends, and discover their strengths.

Run entirely by unpaid volunteers — many of whom are veterans, parents, carers, and community members — our club is fuelled by passion, not profit. We give our time freely, not because we have to, but because we believe in what we're building: a stronger, more inclusive community where every child is seen, supported, and celebrated.

At the heart of our mission is the spirit of service. The same dedication that carried our founders through their naval careers now drives every session, every smile, and every small victory on the field, court, or track.

Our Mission & Values

"Sport Knows No Limits, Neither Do We"

Our club motto was chosen by Rebecca Smith MP. We strive to live these principles in every session:

🤗

Inclusivity

A welcoming environment for all to enjoy sport, without barriers.

🎯

Challenge

Removing boundaries and barriers of ignorance to unlock every child's potential.

🎉

Have Fun

Fun is serious business for children — it helps them problem-solve, socialise and grow.

💛

Kindness

Being friendly, generous and considerate — doing intentional, voluntary acts of kindness to ourselves and others.

🤝

Collaboration

We can achieve more together — our club supports children and each other with pride.

🛡️

Safeguarding First

We have numerous safeguarding policies in place to keep our members, their families and our volunteers safe. Every adult coach and helper is DBS-checked before working with children.

🎗️

Registered Charity

PEM Disability Children's Club is a registered charity, No. 1212204, and a not-for-profit organisation run entirely by unpaid volunteers — supported by kind donations from our community.

Youth Development

Work Experience

We offer work experience opportunities for young people who are passionate about making a difference through sport. By joining our coaching team, youth participants gain hands-on experience working with children with disabilities in a fun, inclusive, and supportive environment. This programme helps build confidence, leadership, and communication skills while promoting empathy and understanding.

Together, we create a positive space where every child — regardless of ability — can experience the joy and benefits of sport. Our youth workers are not DBS-checked or qualified; they are closely monitored and supervised by PEM adult coaches at all times.

Diary of Events

We deliver at least two free sporting sessions per month, plus special one-off events most terms. Most sessions need no booking — just turn up and play, unless marked "by invite only".

Please note: due to weather and other circumstances, sessions and locations may change at short notice — please email if unsure. Unless stated, all sessions run 10:00–12:00 at Plymstock School Astroturf, Church Road, Plymstock, PL9 9AZ.

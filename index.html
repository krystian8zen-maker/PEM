Our Story

Service doesn't end when the uniform comes off.

PEM was founded by Lee Ellis and Luke Monnington, two Royal Navy veterans with over 40 years of combined service. After leaving the military, they set out on a new mission rooted in compassion and community — giving children with disabilities the same chance every child deserves: to play, grow, and make lifelong friends.

Today PEM is a thriving, entirely volunteer-run club providing free, adapted sports sessions and joyful events for pan-disability children and their families across Plymouth.

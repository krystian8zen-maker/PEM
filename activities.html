What We Do

Activities & Sessions

We support the community with equal opportunity and access to sport and recreation. No one should be left behind because of a disability — we remove boundaries, fulfil dreams, enhance self-esteem, and empower youth with disabilities through participation in sport.

We organise sessions in football, basketball, floorball hockey, kwik cricket, mini tennis and soft athletics. We also arrange activities such as litter picks, bouncy castle play days, BBQs, trophy nights and visits to adventure parks when funds allow.

We tend to organise events in the Plymstock, Elburton & Morley areas of Plymouth, but welcome any disabled children and their families from any location. We welcome siblings and parents on the field to support their children if it helps.

We generally meet on the first Sunday of every month (1000–1200) and the second Monday of every month (1700–1800), plus extra ad hoc sessions as opportunity permits. Venues can change, but we tend to use Plymstock School and occasionally Elburton Villa Football Club.

Sports & Activities

⚽ Adapted Football

Small-sided, adapted rules so every child can join in and score a goal.

🏀 Basketball

Indoor sessions at Plymstock School's tented hall, adapted hoops and rules.

🏑 Floorball Hockey

Lightweight sticks and balls make hockey safe and accessible for all.

🏏 Kwik Cricket

Fast-paced, simplified cricket — batting, bowling and fielding for everyone.

🎾 Mini Tennis

Soft balls and smaller courts build racket skills and coordination.

🏃 Soft Athletics

Running, jumping and throwing games designed for every ability level.

🏰 Bouncy Castle Play Days

Seasonal soft-play and bounce days, like our Easter Bounce event.

🧤 Community Litter Picks

Giving back to Plymouth together, building teamwork and pride.

🍔 BBQs & Social Days

Our Summer BBQ & Awards Day brings the whole community together.

🏆 Trophy Nights

Celebrating every child's achievements and effort throughout the year.

🏊 Swimming Trips

Our Autumn excursion often means a splash-filled trip to the pool.

🎢 Adventure Park Visits

Trips to recreation centres and adventure parks when funds allow.

Our Venues

All sessions are 1000–1200 at Plymstock School Astroturf unless stated otherwise. Parking is available at both venues.

Plymstock School

Church Road, Plymstock, Devon, PL9 9AZ

Astroturf, fields, 3G, tennis courts, gyms, parking, toilets, AED on site.

Bus routes: 2 / 4 / Gold / 4A / 4B

Elburton Villa Football Club

Haye Road, Plymouth, PL9 8HS

Used for BBQ multisport days, charity matches and evening events.

Bus routes: 1A / 4 / Gold / 91 / 94
